User Guide

Income User Guide

This is a good platform for knowing your earning and net worth. This platform, ask for the type of your income. If you are a salary earner, weekly-income and daily income will be filtered out. If you earn by wages and allowance, Your daily income and weekly come will not be filtered out. You are going to enter your earning title. That is the way you earn the money eg affiliate marketing, graphic design, content creator,tax officers and whatever occupation you find yourself in. You input your date also. The purpose of inputting your date is it help you calculate your income according to the date for your summaries.Enter the amount of wages or salary that you collect so that it can be calculated. After entering This input, You are going to click on. This is where all operations will be carried out. Your form will be brought out in a tabular form. You can also delete and edit

Expenses User Guide

In this section, you will be asked to input all the details of what you bought so far. The item you bought, The price at which you bought it How many of the product did you buy and the date you bought the product. This is going to calculate The expenses according to the time you bought the product.And that calculations will appear in the expenses summary section.The details of the product you bought will appear in the expenses-details in tarbular form where u can delete and edit After doing that, your overall-balance will be shown in the overall net-balance
